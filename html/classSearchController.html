Detailed Description

Creates FileSearcher + SearchDialog and relays their signals.

Each call to openSearch() spawns a new independent modeless dialog, allowing several concurrent searches in different directories.

The controller emits navigateRequested so MainWindow can forward it to whichever FilePanel is currently active, without the controller needing to know about panels at all (DIP satisfied).
